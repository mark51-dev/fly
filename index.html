About us

The leading private aviation operator and services provider. Delivering world-class services in aircraft sales, completions, management, flight support, charter and FBO. Launched in 1999 in affiliation with US partner NetJets Inc, NasJet originally NetJets Middle East (NJME), demonstrated the highest levels of regional expertise by being the first private company in Saudi Arabia to be awarded an Aircraft Operating Certificate (AOC) by the General Authority of Civil Aviation (GACA) . The company has since grown to managing/supporting in excess of 67 fixed-wing aircraft with a fleet insured value exceeding $2 billion.
